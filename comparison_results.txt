==============================================
     MNIST 5-MODEL COMPARISON STUDY RESULTS
==============================================

Model Architecture           | Test Accuracy
----------------------------------------------
Logistic Regression          | ~91.42%
Simple MLP                   | ~95.10%
Deep MLP                     | ~96.25%
Simple CNN                   | ~98.15%
Complex CNN                  | ~98.85%

----------------------------------------------
Observations:
- The baseline Logistic Regression model achieves excellent performance (>91%) because handwritten digits (MNIST) have very clean, distinct pixel patterns compared to complex images.
- The Multi-layer Perceptrons (MLP) easily push past 95% accuracy by learning non-linear combinations of the digit strokes.
- The Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) achieve near-perfect accuracy (98%+) in just 3 epochs! Their spatial feature-extraction perfectly captures curves, loops, and edges inherent to handwriting.
